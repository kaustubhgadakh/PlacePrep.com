About Us

First Choice For Interview Prepration

PlacePrep is a comprehensive coding interview preparation website designed to help aspiring software engineers ace their interviews with confidence. The platform offers a wide range of features, including curated practice questions, mock interviews with real engineers, video tutorials, and personalized feedback on performance. The website's user-friendly interface enables users to track their progress, identify areas for improvement, and tailor their preparation to specific companies and job roles. With PlacePrep, users can develop essential coding skills and techniques, gain confidence in their abilities, and increase their chances of success in landing their dream job in the tech industry.
